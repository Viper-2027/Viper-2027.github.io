Horse

♥   Pi   ♥

Pi is my dad's horse, he's over twenty years old and is beautiful. He is also the sweetest horse I have ever met. I'm convinced he is a dog trapped in a horse's body! He loves bananas above all else and always knows I have a treat for him. He listens to me even if he gets grumpy about it. I have ridden him before and while he doesn't spook, every time he flicks a fly his entire body moves which can be scary. He is 17 hands tall and weighs approximately 1800 pounds, more than ten times my body weight.

Artistic Swimming

Artistic swimming is the new name for artistic swimming and "combines technical perfection, synchronization, choreography, artistry, and expressive power" as defined by the Olympics. At Mount Holyoke College we have a club called Lyonfish Artistic Swimming, open to any who wish to join! I am the captain and coach of the team and also do all the choreography. This semester at the showcase, we are going to do two songs as a full group, "Glory" by Friday Pilots Club and "Achilles Come Down" by Gang of Youths.

Squash

I have played squash for five years, all four years of high school, and my first year of college. It is the sport I am best at despite the fact I've done other sports for longer. I practice up to 9.5 hours a week and did boot camp this past summer. I recommend the squash classes and am always around to teach! Squash is my passion and I want to share it with the world.
